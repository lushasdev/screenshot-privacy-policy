Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 13, 2025

yorocobu LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Screenshot to Calendar & Notes mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We do not collect traditional personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number unless you voluntarily provide it to us (e.g., when contacting support).

1.2 Device and Usage Information

When you use our App, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, interaction frequency, crash reports, and performance data
  • Local Storage: App preferences and settings stored locally on your device

1.3 Photo and Screenshot Data

Our App accesses your device's photo library and screenshots for the following purposes:

  • Screenshot Detection: The App scans for new screenshots to extract tasks and calendar events
  • Image Processing: Screenshots are processed using AI to identify text, dates, and actionable items
  • Local Processing: Image analysis occurs primarily on your device

Important: Your photos and screenshots are processed locally on your device whenever possible. When cloud processing is necessary (for advanced AI features), images are sent securely to our third-party AI service provider and are not stored permanently.

1.4 Calendar Data

With your permission, the App accesses your device calendar to:

  • Create calendar events from extracted tasks
  • Sync due dates and reminders
  • Read existing calendar entries to avoid duplicates

1.5 Notification Data

We use device notifications to:

  • Alert you when new screenshots are detected
  • Remind you of upcoming tasks and deadlines
  • Notify you when tasks are synced to your calendar

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Core Functionality: Extract tasks and events from screenshots, create calendar entries, and manage your to-do list
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix bugs
  • Personalization: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Background Processing: Automatically scan for new screenshots in the background (when enabled)
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and troubleshoot issues

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 AI Processing

We use Anthropic's Claude AI to process screenshot images and extract text, tasks, and calendar events. When using this service:

  • Screenshots are transmitted securely via encrypted connections
  • Images are processed temporarily and are not permanently stored by Anthropic
  • Anthropic's privacy policy applies to data processed by their service
  • You can learn more at: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

3.2 Analytics and Crash Reporting

We may use third-party analytics services to help us understand how users interact with the App and to identify crashes or errors. These services may collect:

  • Device information and operating system details
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash logs and error reports

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Most of your data, including extracted tasks and calendar information, is stored locally on your device. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you use cloud-dependent features.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication for API communications
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Limited data retention policies

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide the App's functionality:

  • Local Data: Tasks and calendar entries remain on your device until you delete them
  • AI Processing: Screenshots sent to AI services are processed immediately and not stored long-term
  • Crash Reports: Retained for up to 90 days for debugging purposes

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored within the App on your device
  • Delete: Remove individual tasks, calendar entries, or all App data
  • Modify: Edit extracted tasks and calendar events
  • Disable Features: Turn off automatic screenshot scanning, calendar sync, or notifications

6.2 Permissions Management

You can manage App permissions through your device settings:

  • Photo/Media Library access
  • Calendar access
  • Notification permissions
  • Background app refresh

Revoking these permissions may limit or disable certain App features.

6.3 Data Portability

You can export your tasks and calendar data from the App at any time through the App's export functionality.

6.4 Rights for EU/EEA Residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

6.5 Rights for California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (Note: We do not sell your personal information)
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Union). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy
  • Sending an in-app notification for material changes

Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from your jurisdiction. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States and other countries where our service providers operate.

10. Do Not Track

Some web browsers have a "Do Not Track" feature. Because there is not yet a common understanding of how to interpret Do Not Track signals, our App does not currently respond to browser Do Not Track signals.
